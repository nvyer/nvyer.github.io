Our Mission

Forest Therapy is a research-based framework for supporting healing and wellness through immersion in forests and other natural environments. Forest Therapy is inspired by the Japanese practice of Shinrin-Yoku, which translates to "forest bathing." Studies have demonstrated
a wide array of health benefits, especially in the cardiovascular and immune systems, and for stabilizing and improving mood and cognition.
